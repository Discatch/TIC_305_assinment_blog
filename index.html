K-POP WORLD DOMINATION

by Kristel Dela Cruz

Annyeong! If you are one of those who want to enter the K-pop community fandom, this blog got you! This guide helps you appreciate K-pop even more, and integrate yourself into K-community because once you enter there’s no turning back

Well, I'm not surprised why you decided to enter the fandom, because global K-Pop fans have been increasing around the world for a good number of years now (I have been a fan since 2017). So I want you to welcome right away to the K-pop stardom.

A LITTLE CRUMBS TO ENTER THE FANDOM

You may encounter the word "FANDOM" at the beginning of this article, fandom refers to the fanbase of an artist. In western culture Directioner from 1Direction, Swifties from Taylor Swift, Believer from Justin Bieber and the likes are the example of the word fandom in western pop music culture. In the K-pop community, the word fandom may be difficult to explain because there are sub-unit within a certain group and there is also a solo debut for each member of the group. 

For example, the fandom name of EXO is EXO-L. EXO then debuted a single artist within the group. Soondingies for Chen and Erikyoongs for Baekhyun.

EXO Baekhyun latest single

EXO Chen latest single

    Yes, being involved in a group with many individual components seems too tricky. But a k-pop fan is recognized as one of the most good at memorizing pieces of information. There is a bad side of K-pop fandom, some fans are backslashing other groups to make their favorite group arise to stardom. On the other hand, there are hundreds of fandoms that support other idols, create fundraising, charity, and outreach program under the name of the group that they are supporting.

    That is a piece of brief information because discussing all the aspects of the k-community is too deep and wide. There are fandom names, fandom colors, merchandise, and fan chants of a certain group. Entering a fandom will help you make friends with other people that have the same interest as you. Do not let other people define you as a mere fan of a group you're more than just a fan that inspires because of them, love unconditionally, and appreciate other people truly. 

K-POP AND KOREAN GOVERNMENT

BTS with President Moon Jae In

      South Korea is well known for taking the music industry seriously. The Korean Government supports this field because it affects the economic growth of the country.

      Asia saw a massive financial recession back in the late 1990s. The decision to use music to boost its reputation and create a cultural impact has been brought on by the South Korean Government. Eventually, millions of dollars were allocated to a Ministry of Culture that comes with devotedly supporting K-pop.

K-pop boy band EXO welcomes Trump and his daughter Ivanka with signed albums

      Government invites the Korean Group to represent the nation in a political event. The government invited a nation's pick boy/girl group because K-pop music is one of the futures of South Korea. An event such as this should be held diplomatically for the future of the country. It is mart action to put Korean group to become ambassadors that spread good relationship and diplomatic individual.
